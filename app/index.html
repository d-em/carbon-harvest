Introducing BiotronTower™

The BiotronTower™ from Carbon Harvest is a unique, bolt-on, addition to your flue-gas emission source that literally turns your waste emissions into environmentally friendly, saleable biomass.

Down arrow

Just add CO₂

Bolted onto your existing flue system, the BiotronTower™ captures and uses carbon dioxide and nitrogen oxide emissions to produce valuable algal biomass.

Minimal impact – maximum benefits

Land and water requirements are minimal meaning that the BiotronTower™ can be situated close to the emission source as a scalable, standalone bio-refinery that operates reliably 24 hours a day, 7 days a week all year round.

Current estimates indicate that two tonnes of CO₂ flue emissions will produce one tonne of saleable dry biomass. Production at this rate will lock up 0.5 tonnes of carbon whilst releasing 1 tonne of oxygen into the atmosphere – greatly enhancing your corporate social responsibility.

That’s a lot of algae right? Algae is pretty useful stuff though – see how it’s used…

Down arrow

Output

Microalgae biomass is rich in protein (up to 70%), antioxidants, carbohydrates (5—15%) and fatty acids (10—20%). Algal biomass also provides Omega-3 (EPA+DHA) required by farmed fish.

That means that dry algae biomass meal can be used as a protein supplement for use in animal and fish feeds. Algae biomass can also be used for human food – ever heard of spirulina? The spirulina in your morning smoothie contains 3 x the amount of usable protein as beef! Plant based meat is big business but algae biomass is also a large part of the cosmetics industry and is also utilised in the manufacture of bio-degradable plastics and even bio-jet fuel.

Who are we?

Fair question. Carbon Harvest Ltd was set up to monetise nearly 20 years of research and development into driving photosynthesis in algae enclosed in a three-dimensional container.

So - we›ve been at this for a long time…

The BiotronTower™ Technology is the culmination of this research and is configured as a high technology, precision controlled, ultra-high productivity system designed by applying advanced science, engineering, processing and marketing principles designed to provide industrial scale biomass production at commercially acceptable Return-on-Investment.
